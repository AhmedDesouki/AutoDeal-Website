AutoDeals

is an Egyptian showroom that targets to sell exotic and fancy cars to their customers. AutoDeals have managed to leave a tremendous mark in the Egyptian automotive market with its full range of super and exotic cars.

AutoDeals have been sharing a legacy full of achievements with their customers for almost 40 years. AutoDeals was found on January, 12, 1980 headquartering in the Egyptian city of Cairo. AutoDeals targeted the exotic automotive and how it was weak back then in Egypt and we made our best letting the world know about our cars. On June 16th 2001 AutoDeals started the journey of selling exotic cars and became the first exotic automotive in Egypt. Since then AutoDeals took steps that led us to great success year after year.

AutoDeals have positioned itself as one of the top 10 showrooms in the middle east making sure that our quality is tremendous and that our products are safe and has the greatest functions our customer could ask for.
